Elina Ivnytska
Photographer from Spokane, WA

I’ve been doing photography for over 5 years. For me, photographing is not a job but a passion. I love people, I think everyone is unique and beautiful in their own way, so I try to do everything to show their beauty in the photo. I work in Spokane, but is always ready to leave the city to shoot in other places. The more interesting you have an idea, the more likely we are to work together. Therefore, text me, do not hesitate. I’m always open to new friends.
